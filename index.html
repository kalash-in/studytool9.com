Study Tips

  • Break down your study material into smaller, manageable parts.
  • Use active recall and spaced repetition for better retention.
  • Take regular breaks using techniques like the Pomodoro method.
  • Teach someone else what you've learned to reinforce your knowledge.
  • Stay organized with a planner or digital tool to track assignments and deadlines.

Useful Websites

  • Khan Academy - Free online courses and lessons in various subjects.
  • Coursera - Online courses from top universities.
  • edX - Learn from global universities and organizations.
  • Quizlet - Flashcards and study tools for students.
  • Wolfram Alpha - Computational knowledge engine, great for math and science.

Online Tools

  • Google Drive - Cloud storage for all your documents and projects.
  • Trello - Project management and to-do list tool.
  • Notion - All-in-one workspace for notes, tasks, and wikis.
  • Focus@Will - Focused music to help you concentrate while studying.
  • Canva - Graphic design tool for creating study materials and presentations.
